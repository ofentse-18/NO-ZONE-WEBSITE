Size Guide

Since every NO ZONE piece is upcycled and one-of-a-kind, sizing can vary. Use the measurements below as a general guide. If unsure, Contact Us before ordering.

All measurements are in centimetres (cm). Our pieces are designed for a relaxed, streetwear fit.

Tip: When in doubt, size up. Our pieces are designed for a relaxed drop at a time.
